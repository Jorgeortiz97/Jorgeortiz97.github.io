Hello everyone!

My name is Jorge Ortiz Escribano and I'm a Computer Engineer. In this blog, I will share some of the most important software projects in which I have worked on. My blog is welcoming to new and experienced programmers alike. Hope you enjoy it!


Do you want to know more?

I was born in Murcia in 1997. I started programming for the first time when I was only 11 years old. At that time, I liked to create homemade software for the PSP video game console (usually called 'homebrew'). I have already posted about that. From that moment, I became interested in programming and it is still one of the most important activities of my life.

From 2015 to 2019, I was studying the Computer Engineering degree from the University of Murcia. During that time, I was an internal student in the Department of Computer Engineering and Technology (DITEC). In addition, one of my projects was endowed with a Collaboration Grant from the Spanish Ministry of Education and Culture (MEC). I have also done some internships, collaborated with some companies and had a full-time job. But my dream is to become a university professor someday...

In addition to programming, I also like to practice sports, especifically, running. If you too, don't hesitate to follow me on Endomondo.
